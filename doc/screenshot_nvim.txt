================================================================================
								*screenshot.nvim

This plugin is a wrapper around the carbon-now cli program which allows you to
take pretty screenshots of your leaving without leaving neovim. See examples
below:

                                                         *screenshot_nvim.SS()
screenshot_nvim.SS()
    Take screenshot of all the text in the current buffer and either save it
    to your clipboard or on your specified folder depending on the options you
    have setup.


                                                         *screenshot_nvim.SSText()
screenshot_nvim.SSText()
    Take screenshot of all the highlited text in the current buffer and either save it
    to your clipboard or on your specified folder depending on the options you
    have setup.
